Sumaira Isaacs
COO
MCI Global (IMEA)


Profile

A creative juggernaut who has weaved her magic in everything that she has explored... from glamour to travel from arts to events, she has excelled in all. Presenting the woman of substance……the dynamic, practical, lovable - SUMAIRA KAMRAN ISAACS With a mission to deliver the best and a passion to outshine the rest, this vibrant personality with her sheer perseverance and hard work has scaled the ladder of success to now become a robust business power woman.


For one who started off with humble beginnings of a simple ticketing supervisor she propelled herself to be a general manager of Net Tours and managed a Tourism Operating Company. She moved strength by strength taking on Arabian adventures management for Emirates Airlines leading a team of over 40 employees, within an organisation of over 400 staff.


Sumaira let her artistic juices flow along the way with Kenza Art Gallery. A resident artist and a gallery director she owns this vibrant art arcade. From being responsible for all the strategic management, planning, positioning of the Art Gallery and its business, she ensures that it maintains a key position within the local art scene.


Presently she is the backbone of MCI in this region, as COO IMEA (India, Middle East, Africa) of a business that was started from scratch in partnership; it has now turned into a local success story. Sumaira has been responsible for the Strategic management and leading a team of over 90 full time staff spread over Dubai, India and Canada, monitoring the day to day operations, business development, and bottom line results of the company. MCI is one of the reputable conference and meetings organiser in the world.


With so many features on her cap, this go-getter entrepreneur still feels that there are more dreams to achieve and more goals to attain.
