Mr. Andrew Cleaves
IDD National Express Board Director lead for Transport


Profile

Andrew Cleaves is the Managing Director of National Express' International Division, bidding for transport networks across the Middle East and Europe. Recent submissions include Riyadh and Bahrain. Previously Andrew was MD of National Express UK Coach, having joined the business at the end of August 2010. Prior to this Andrew had been with Tube Lines since August 2000 and rose through senior commercial roles to be appointed Chief Executive in February 2010. Before Tube Lines, he worked in Sales and Marketing for Otis plc and with PA Consulting. He has an MBA from Cranfield School of Management as well as an MA from Cambridge University. Andrew was born in Bristol but his home is now in Bishop's Stortford. Outside of work he is a passionate cricket fan and a supporter of Bristol City Football Club.
