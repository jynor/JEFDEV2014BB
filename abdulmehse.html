Profile

Abdulmohsen is the General Manager of Prince Ahmed Bin Salman Applied Media Academy since January 2013, he was the founder of DLinks Advisory ( a Business Development advisory), also he is founding member and a Board Member of Al-Ghad Youth Foundation (the first Youth NGO in Saudi Arabia,), he is also the founder of "InvestINSaudi" company ( Bought later by Saudi Arabian General Investment Authority ( SAGIA) in 2005.


Abdulmohsen was a cofounder and Chief Executive Officer of the Global Competitiveness Forum (GCF) 2006-2011. At the Saudi Arabian General Investment Authority ( SAGIA) under the GCF he was also leading several initiatives such as the Responsible Competitiveness, the Saudi Fast Growth Companies.


He started his career in the private sector at Almarai company in 1997 and acquired many positions in finance and business development. During that time, he had been awarded the prestigious British Chevening Scholarship to pursue his postgraduate studies. Abdulmohsen obtained his master degree in the field of International Business Management from Manchester University, His international business career developed also through executive programs in Management & Leadership at IMD and Columbia University.


He authored tow books "Impacts of Globalization on Saudi Arabian Dairy Industry, With Highlight To Foreign Direct Investments", and "Saudi Companies & Social Responsibility" in 2006. In addition, he is a columnist in Al-Eqtisadiah Newspaper the Aljazirah Newspaper targeting issues in business development, Socio-economics and investments. Abdulamohsen is an active member of many NGOs and associations locally and internationally.
