H.E. Mattar Al Tayer

H.E. Mattar Al Tayer
Chairman of the Board and Executive Director
The Roads & Transport Authority (RTA), Dubai, UAE


Profile

In September 2011, His Highness Sheikh Mohammed bin Rashid Al Maktoum, UAE Vice- President and Prime Minister and Ruler of Dubai, bestowed the "Mohammed bin Rashid Sash" upon Mattar Al Tayer in recognition of his outstanding achievements in the Roads and Transport Authority (RTA).


In November 2005, H. H. Sheikh Mohammed Bin Rashid Al Maktoum, Vice President, UAE Prime Minister and Ruler of Dubai appointed Mr. Al Tayer as the Chairman of the Board and Executive Director of the newly established Roads and Transport Authority. Since then, Mr. Al Tayer has been the driving force behind the remarkable achievements of RTA which have turned it into a world-class transportation organization.


In his capacity as the Chairman of the Board and Executive Director of RTA, Mr. Al Tayer has been responsible for fulfilling Dubai's transportation needs including roads, buses, metro, marine, Intelligent Transportation Systems and transportation policies and legislations. He has managed to establish a strong managerial and professional base of human capital and administrative systems to ensure that RTA meets the increasing transportation demands of Dubai. In this regard, RTA's vision of "Safe and Smooth Transport for All" reflects RTA's commitment to providing integrated transportation solutions that exceed the public's expectations and meet the development plans and the mobility needs of the Emirate of Dubai. Under Al Tayer's Leadership, RTA completed several new road projects including the Business Bay Crossing, Al Garhoud Bridge and the floating bridge. This was in addition to the enhancements of several major roads and intersections originally built under Al Tayer's supervision during his tenure with Dubai Municipality. These included Sheikh Zayed Road, Emirates Road as well as Al Khail and Ras Al Khour Roads. Moreover, Al Tayer led RTA's team to fundamentally improve the public transport network in Dubai with several projects including Dubai Metro, Al Sufouh Tram and the expansion of the bus and marine networks. Under his supervision, RTA completed projects with a total budget exceeding 65 Billion Dirhams.


Before chairing the Board of RTA, Mr. Al Tayer was the Deputy Director General of Dubai Municipality and prior to that he was the Assistant Director General of the Municipality responsible for the Roads and General Projects Sector from 1994 to 2005. From 1983 to 1994 Mr. Al Tayer was promoted from a trainee engineer to the Director of the Roads Department in a few short years. And during his tenure with Dubai Municipality, Mr. Al Tayer supervised most of Dubai's road projects and numerous vitally important building projects with a total combined budget of approximately 20 Billion Dirhams.
