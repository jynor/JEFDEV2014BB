Profile

Mr Lim Boon Tiong is presently the General Manager of ITE Education Services (ITEES), a fully-owned subsidiary and consulting arm of the Institute of Technical Education (ITE), Singapore. As a General Manager of ITEES since Jul 2010, Mr Lim oversees the entire operations of the company whose core activity is to administer and implement training and consultancy projects with overseas institutions in the field of Technical and Vocational Education and Training (TVET).


Besides managing the day-to-day operations, Mr Lim is also directly involved as Project Director for all major TVET projects in various countries such as India, Myanmar, Nigeria, Thailand, Philippines, Cambodia, Indonesia, Laos, China, Vietnam and Malaysia. The value of each of these projects ranges from US$300,000 to US$3.8 million. Under the management of Mr Lim, the company's revenue grew at an average of 20% annually while net profits jumped 4-fold from $395k in FY09 to a projected $1.87m in FY2013.


Prior to joining ITEES, Mr Lim was the Manager at ITE managing ITE's collaborations with industry, government-to-government consultancy projects, industry-based training programmes and commercialisation of ITE's intellectual properties such as staff inventions and literary works. All in all, Mr Lim has 22 years of experience in the field of TVET, of which, the last 12 years were in project management for TVET-related projects. Mr Lim is well-versed with the development of TVET in Singapore and across Asia.


In 2003, Mr Lim pioneered the setting up of ITE Holding Pte Ltd which in turn incorporated ITEES which Mr Lim is currently overseeing. Today, ITEES is a fast growing company offering TVET consultancy and training services to public institutions, government agencies and private organisations in more than 20 countries. 5 Mr Lim is an Engineer by training. He graduated with a Bachelor of Engineering (Honours) Degree in Electrical Engineering from the National University of Singapore in 1991.
