Mr. Fareed Karmostaji

Mr. Fareed Karmostaji
Director of the Financing Department
Dubai SME


Profile

Fareed Karmostaji is an accomplished professional with extensive experience in developing and leading transformational public sector initiatives in entrepreneurship development, customer relations, and revenue management in Dubai. Since July 2013, he has been serving as Managing Director of the Mohammad Bin Rashid Fund for SME (The Fund), a subsidiary of the Mohammad Bin Rashid Establishment for SME Development (Dubai SME), mandated to promote entrepreneurship and create a strong growth environment for small and medium enterprises (SMEs) in Dubai.


Prior to current position in the Fund, Fareed was Director of Entrepreneur Development Department at the Dubai SME, also in charge of Export Market Development at the Export Development Corporation (EDC) of Dubai. In this role he contributed to the strategy plan of EDC and was instrumental in pursuing and co-ordinating export opportunities for Dubai businesses through research, market intelligence and establishment of offices overseas.


Fareed has also provided valuable leadership in customer service and asset management as Director at the Roads and Transport Authority (RTA) in Dubai during 2006-2008. As Director of Customer Service Centres, Fareed oversaw the establishment of new service delivery networks and channels for RTA in line with the government excellence standards in Dubai and international best practices. He also led the creation of a Real Estate Investment Department at RTA and as its Director, developed new concepts for optimising the value of RTA's real estate assets.


A graduate in Business Administration from the UAE University, Fareed started his career with a local bank and within a year, was appointed as Accountant/Auditor in the Department of Economic Development (DED) in Dubai. Fareed and his team were instrumental in bringing in a series of innovations to streamline revenue collection, auditing and co-ordination with businesses in DED's Local Fees and Treasury sections. His sharp focus on innovation played a stellar role in Fareed becoming an Administrative Officer, Head of SME Development and later a Director, Business Development at DED.


An avid golfer and snooker player, Fareed also enjoys travelling, photography and reading. He speaks fluent English and Russian, in addition to his mother tongue, Arabic.
