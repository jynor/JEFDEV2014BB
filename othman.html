Profile

H.E. Eng. Abdullatif bin Ahmed Al Othman was appointed as Governor of the Saudi Arabian General Investment Authority on the May18, 2012 . Prior to assuming this role, H.E. Al Othman spent more than three decades at Saudi Aramco in various distinctive leadership and executive positions. His tenure with Saudi Aramco allowed him extensive experience in the fields of engineering, project management, planning, marketing, public relations and finance. Throughout his career, H.E. Al Othman was known for his building institutional excellence through developing and promoting human capital, institutional knowledge and cultivating teamwork. Practical Experience (1981 – present)


1981 – 2001

H.E. Eng. Al Othman advanced his project management career by directly participating in the execution of a number of mega-projects implemented by Saudi Aramco. These projects include Gas Gathering Project Phase II; Marjan Oil Field Project; and almost all Refinery Modernization Programs for Ras Tanura as well as studies for Qasim and Al Shuqaiq .


2001 – 2003

H.E. Eng. Al Othman gained valuable experience in distinct fields. In particular, he worked on strategic planning, cost management and international sales. In September, 2001 he was appointed as Vice President of Saudi Aramco in charge of Corporate affairs and Public Relations.


2003 – 2012

In 2003, H.E. Eng. Al Othman joined the Finance Organization as Vice President of Finance and in 2005 was promoted as Saudi Aramco's Senior Vice President of Finance. During his tenure in Finance, he led the organization through a major transformation and also established a specialized department for funding joint venture and acquisition projects. On August 29, 2011, he was appointed as Saudi Aramco's Senior Vice President for Engineering and Project Management.


2012 - present

H.E. Eng. Al Othman is currently serving his term as theGovernor and Chairman of the Board of Directors of the Saudi Arabian General Investment Authority and Chairman of the Economic Cities Authority. Committee Membership and Other Positions
